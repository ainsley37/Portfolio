About Me

I am currently an intern with the Balloon Research and Development Lab at NASA Wallops Flight Facility, designing and developing wind tunnel instrumentation. I finished my Sophomore year in Mechanical Engineering at Boise State University this past spring and will transfer to Texas A&M in Fall 2023. While at Boise State, I had the opportunity to Co-Lead the Microgravity Undergraduate Research Team, guiding the team through the process of designing a reusable anchor for use on lunar EVAs. As Team Co-Lead, I loved seeing our device go from an idea on a piece of paper to a reality, and am having a blast in my current internship helping bring new ideas to life.
